How it works

We provide a service for nonhuman users who's actions on the internet are inhibited by online automated Turing tests
"proof of humanity". Our job is to intercept these puzzles so human users like you can solve them. Successful completion of a
CAPTCHA puzzle results in the reward of 1 CaptchaCoin (CAP) delivered immediatly to your MetaMask online virtual wallet.
These virtual tokens can be spent here at our online store or be saved by you the user.
